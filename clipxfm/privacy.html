ClipXfm – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

ClipXfm ("the App") is a desktop utility that transforms clipboard text using AI. This policy explains what data the App handles and how.

Data the App Processes

When you trigger an AI action, the text currently in your clipboard is sent to the AI provider you have configured (Google Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic, or a local LLM). No other personal data is collected or transmitted by the App itself.

AI Providers

Clipboard text is sent directly from your device to the provider's API. The App developer does not receive, store, or process this data. Please review the privacy policy of your chosen provider:

  • Google (Gemini)
  • OpenAI
  • Anthropic

If you use a local LLM (e.g. LM Studio, Ollama), data stays on your device and is never sent to external servers.

API Keys

API keys are stored exclusively in your operating system's secure credential store (macOS Keychain / Windows Credential Manager). They are never transmitted to the App developer or any third party.

Analytics & Telemetry

The App collects no analytics, crash reports, or telemetry of any kind.
